Permit: Event Permit
Description: Required for hosting public events
Application Deadline: 30 days before the event
Contact: City Hall, permits@cityhall.com

Permit: Food Stall Permit
Description: Required for vendors selling food
Application Deadline: 15 days before the event
Contact: Health Department, health@cityhall.com

Permit: Music Performance License
Description: Required for live music performances
Application Deadline: 20 days before the event
Contact: Music Licensing Board, music@cityhall.com

Regulation: Noise Control
Description: Must adhere to city noise ordinances
Details: Maximum sound level of 70 dB after 10 PM
Contact: City Hall, noisecontrol@cityhall.com
